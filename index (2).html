Company

Built to close the gap between ICT and the ground it stands on.

Established in 2019 to meet the nation's demand for construction, development, supply, installation, and maintenance works — particularly in the general and telecommunication sectors.

ICON Enterprise is a Dhaka-based proprietorship driven by innovative trends in technology, delivering end-to-end solutions with a budget-friendly approach — and the discipline of public procurement behind every contract.
